Teng Wang earned his master's degree from the Erasmus Mundus program in Dynamics of Renewables-based Power Systems (DREAM) in 2024 after completing his bachelor's at Sichuan University, China. He is a PhD student at North Carolina State University working on transmission-distribution co-simulation. His interests span analyzing renewable-based power system dynamics, modelling power system using tools such as ANDES, OpenDSS.
